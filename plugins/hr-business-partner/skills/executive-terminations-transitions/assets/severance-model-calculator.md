# Severance Model Calculator (Guidelines)

| Component | Formula | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cash | Base salary * X months (обычно 6-12) | $450K base * 9 = $337.5K |
| Bonus | Pro-rated bonus на дату выхода | 60% target * 0.5 year = $135K |
| Equity | Accelerated vesting (e.g., 12 мес) | 12/48 of unvested RSUs |
| Benefits | Healthcare continuation (6-12 мес) | $2K/mo * 12 = $24K |
| Outplacement | Fixed fee | $25K |

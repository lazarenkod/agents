## Pre-Meeting Setup

### Подготовка помещения и участников

```
Локация: Приватный конференц-зал
- НЕ офис увольняемого топ-менеджера
- Нейтральная территория
- Звуконепроницаемое помещение
- Без окон в коридор (конфиденциальность)

Участники:
- CEO (или Board Chair для CEO termination)
- HR Director или Chief People Officer
- General Counsel (при legal risks)
- Optional: Board member (при увольнении C-level)

Timing:
- День: Вторник или среда (НЕ пятница - оставляет weekend для обработки)
- Время: 10:00-11:00 (не начало дня, не конец)
- Длительность: 30-45 минут (выделить 1 час)
```

### Материалы (Подготовить до встречи)

```
□ Separation Agreement (напечатан, подписан legal)
□ Severance Summary (1-page, крупный шрифт)
□ Return of Property Checklist
  - Laptop, phone, badge, credit card
  - Access keys, security tokens
  - Company car (if applicable)
□ IT Access Revocation Plan (координация с IT)
□ Security escort instructions (if needed)
□ Box of tissues (seriously)
```

### Coordination Timeline

```
Day Before:
16:00 - Legal final review separation agreement
17:00 - IT prepared to disable access (on standby)
17:30 - Security briefed (if sensitive exit)
18:00 - Calendar invite sent ("1-on-1 with CEO" - не раскрывать)

Morning Of:
08:00 - Final check: All materials ready
09:00 - Brief HR and Legal on script
09:30 - Position security (if needed)
09:45 - Final go/no-go decision
10:00 - Meeting begins
```

## Opening: Delivering the Message

### The First 60 Seconds (Critical)

**Скрипт:**

```
CEO: "[Name], спасибо что пришли. [HR Director] и [General Counsel] тоже
здесь, чтобы поддержать этот разговор.

У меня сложная новость. После тщательного обсуждения с [Board/leadership],
мы приняли решение сделать leadership change. Ваша позиция [Title]
устраняется с эффективной датой [Date].

Это финальное решение."

[ПАУЗА - дайте им поглотить информацию. 10-15 секунд молчания.]
```

### Что НЕ говорить в opening

```
✗ "Я ужасно себя чувствую, делая это..."
  → Делает вас слабым, смещает фокус на вас

✗ "Это было очень сложное решение..."
  → Звучит как извинение, подрывает ясность

✗ "Board заставил меня сделать это..."
  → Перекладывание ответственности, unprofessional

✗ "У нас нет выбора..."
  → Неправда, у вас всегда есть выбор

✗ "Ничего личного, это бизнес..."
  → Cliché, неуважительно
```

### Что НУЖНО говорить

```
✓ Прямо и ясно: "Ваша позиция устраняется"
✓ Финальность: "Это решение финальное"
✓ Дата: "С эффективной датой [Date]"
✓ Пауза: Дайте время поглотить
```

## Providing Context

### Framing by Exit Type

#### Type 1: Performance-Based Exit

**Скрипт:**

```
CEO: "Это решение базируется на performance. Несмотря на coaching и
поддержку в течение последних [timeframe], мы не увидели прогресса,
который нам нужен в [specific areas]:

- [Specific performance gap 1 with objective data]
  Пример: 'Revenue team miss target на 30% три квартала подряд'

- [Specific performance gap 2]
  Пример: 'Attrition вашей команды составил 45% за год (company avg: 12%)'

- [Specific performance gap 3]
  Пример: 'Board потерял confidence после [specific event]'

Мы верим, что нужен fresh leadership для достижения наших целей."
```

**Key Principles:**
- Specific, не vague ("underperformance" → конкретные метрики)
- Objective data (числа, факты, не opinions)
- Reference prior coaching (не surprise)

#### Type 2: Culture Fit / Leadership Style Mismatch

**Скрипт:**

```
CEO: "Это решение о strategic fit. После наблюдения за [timeframe],
стало ясно, что есть mismatch между вашим leadership style и нашей
company culture.

Specifically:

- [Culture principle] требует [expected behavior], но мы наблюдали
  [actual behavior]
  Пример: 'Наш принцип 'Collaborate & Build Trust' требует open
  communication, но feedback от команды указывает на defensive и
  dismissive style в meetings'

- [Specific incident that violated values]
  Пример: 'В последнем All-Hands вы publicly criticized product team,
  что нарушило наш принцип 'Respect & Psychological Safety'

Это не о performance — ваши результаты [acknowledge positives].
Это о том, что leadership approach не совместим с тем, как мы хотим
вести компанию."
```

**Key Principles:**
- Не blame personality, focus на behaviors
- Tie to company values (documented)
- Acknowledge contributions (не totally negative)

#### Type 3: Restructuring / Strategic Shift

**Скрипт:**

```
CEO: "Это решение бизнеса, не reflection на ваш performance.

[Explain business context]:
- 'Мы приняли решение exit [business line] и focus на [new direction]'
- 'После acquisition, мы consolidate [functions]'
- 'Due to budget constraints, мы делаем executive team changes'

В результате этого strategic shift, [Title] role больше не существует
в нашей structure.

Я хочу быть абсолютно ясным: Это не о вашей работе. Ваши contributions
в [achievements] были significant. Это просто business decision."
```

**Key Principles:**
- Lead with business rationale (не personal)
- Be explicit: "NOT about performance"
- Acknowledge contributions generously
- Offer strongest severance (business decision = more generous)

#### Type 4: Misconduct / Ethics Violation

**Скрипт:**

```
CEO: "Мы недавно стали aware of [allegations/findings] связанных с
[misconduct type].

После thorough investigation [by legal/external investigator], мы
concluded что произошло violation of [policy/law/ethics standard].

Specifically:

- [Finding 1 with evidence]
  Пример: 'Investigation found 5 instances of inappropriate comments
  to direct reports, confirmed by witnesses'

- [Finding 2]
  Пример: 'Review of expense reports revealed unauthorized personal
  charges totaling $47,000'

Это составляет termination for cause under вашего employment contract.
Ваше employment terminated effective immediately."

[Hand over to Legal]

General Counsel: "Я здесь чтобы review legal aspects и ваш separation
terms. В случае for-cause termination..."
```

**Key Principles:**
- Legal present (MUST)
- State findings, не allegations
- Reference investigation (shows due process)
- Hand over to legal quickly (это legal matter)
- Minimal severance, immediate termination

## Severance Details

### Presenting the Severance Package

**HR Director takes over:**

```
HR Director: "Давайте review ваш separation package. Вот письменный summary.

[Hand over Severance Summary document]

Key terms:

1. SEVERANCE PAY:
   - [X months] base salary continuation
   - Total amount: $[XXX,XXX]
   - Payment schedule: [Monthly/Lump sum], starting [date]
   - Условие: Signing release of claims

2. EQUITY:
   - Unvested equity: [Treatment - accelerate/forfeit]
   - [X] additional months of vesting credit
   - Exercise period: Extended to [X months] post-termination
   - Total equity value (at current price): $[XXX,XXX]

3. BONUS:
   - [Current year bonus treatment - pro-rated/forfeited/discretionary]
   - Amount: $[XX,XXX] or N/A
   - Payment date: [Date or 'Not applicable']

4. BENEFITS:
   - Health insurance: COBRA + [X months] company-paid
   - Life insurance: Continues through [date]
   - 401k: Vested portion available, [vesting schedule]
   - Other benefits: [PTO payout, etc.]

5. SUPPORT:
   - Outplacement services: [Provider], up to $[XX,XXX]
   - Career coaching: [X sessions with executive coach]
   - Reference: [Type of reference - neutral/positive]

TOTAL PACKAGE VALUE: $[XXX,XXX] + equity

Это generous package базируется на [rationale: years of service,
performance, business decision, contractual terms]."
```

### Explaining What You Need in Return

```
HR Director: "В exchange за этот severance package, мы просим вас:

1. RELEASE OF CLAIMS:
   - Вы соглашаетесь не подавать litigation против компании
   - Это covers wrongful termination, discrimination, etc.
   - Standard practice для separation agreements

2. RETURN OF PROPERTY:
   - All company property: laptop, phone, badge, credit card, etc.
   - By [date - обычно today или end of week]

3. CONFIDENTIALITY:
   - Company confidential information остается confidential
   - Trade secrets, customer data, strategic plans
   - Continues indefinitely

4. NON-DISPARAGEMENT (MUTUAL):
   - Вы не будете делать negative statements о компании
   - Мы не будем делать negative statements о вас
   - Applies to social media, press, conversations
   - This is MUTUAL - защищает обе стороны

5. COOPERATION:
   - Available для knowledge transfer [X hours over Y weeks]
   - May need testimony если litigation (unrelated to your departure)
   - Reasonable cooperation with transition

6. NON-COMPETE / NON-SOLICIT (если applicable):
   - [Review specific terms from contract]
   - Duration: [X months]
   - Geography: [Scope]
   - Enforcement: [Terms]

У вас есть [21-45 дней] чтобы review это с вашим attorney before signing.
Это стандартный срок."
```

### Handling Immediate Questions on Severance

**Common questions and responses:**

```
Q: "Это negotiable?"
A: "Этот package базируется на [contract/policy/years of service].
   Пожалуйста, review с вашим attorney. Если у них есть questions,
   они могут contact [Legal contact]. Мы открыты для reasonable
   discussions."

Q: "Почему только [X] months? Я думал [Y] months."
A: "Этот package follows [contract Section X / standard practice /
   Board-approved formula]. Но please review и если ваш attorney
   видит issues, мы можем discuss."

Q: "Что если я не подпишу?"
A: "Это ваше right не подписывать. Если вы не подпишете, вы получите
   только minimum contractual obligations: [X]. Этот package более
   generous потому что мы хотим amicable separation."

Q: "Можете ли вы увеличить severance до [X]?"
A: "Я понимаю вашу просьбу. Давайте вы review package с attorney,
   и мы можем have follow-up discussion. Я не могу commit сейчас
   без Board approval."
```

**DO NOT negotiate in this meeting. Always defer to legal review.**

```

   - Ваш access к email, Slack, systems будет disabled [timeframe:

   - Это standard security protocol, не personal

2. COMPANY PROPERTY:
   - Please return [list]: laptop, phone, badge, credit card
   - [Security/HR] will assist с packing personal items
   - Если нужно больше времени для personal items, мы можем arrange

3. TRANSITION:
   - [If applicable]: Вы будете available для knowledge transfer
     [X hours/week for Y weeks, per consulting agreement]
   - [Your direct reports/key stakeholders] will be informed [today/tomorrow]

```

## Handling Difficult Reactions

### Reaction Type 1: Anger / Hostility

**Scenario:**
```
Executive: "Это bullshit! Я built эту fucking компанию! Вы делаете огромную ошибку!"
[Stands up, агрессивный body language]
```

**Response Script:**

```
CEO: [Calm, steady voice, не defensive]

"[Name], я понимаю, вы upset. Это очень difficult conversation.

[Pause - 5 seconds]

Давайте take a breath.

[If они садятся]: Спасибо. Я знаю это shocking news.

[If они остаются стоять]: Пожалуйста, садитесь. Давайте finish этот
conversation respectfully.

Решение final, но мы committed к respectful transition и generous
support для вас.

Ваши contributions to [Company] were real и appreciated. Это не diminishes
того, что вы built."

[If anger continues]

"Я need вас remain professional. Если вы не можете, мы end этот meeting
сейчас и reconvene tomorrow когда вы готовы discuss details calmly.

Что вы хотите?"
```

**If Escalates to Threats:**

```
Executive: "I'll fucking destroy this company! I'll go to press! I'll sue!"

CEO: [Stand up, serious tone]

"[Name], я понимаю you're extremely upset. Но я не могу continue
эту conversation если есть threats.

Вы имеете legal rights, и если вы choose pursue legal action,
это ваш choice.

Но этот conversation ends now. [HR Director] will follow up with
you tomorrow to discuss details.

Please leave the building сейчас."

[Signal to Security if needed]
```

**Post-Meeting Actions for Anger:**
- Document everything immediately (exact words, threats)
- Notify Security (potential workplace violence risk)
- Legal review (assess litigation risk, potential restraining order)
- Brief executive team (safety protocol)

### Reaction Type 2: Tears / Emotional Breakdown

**Scenario:**
```
Executive: [Starts crying, unable to speak]
"I... I can't believe this... My family... I just... [breaks down]"
```

**Response Script:**

```
CEO: [Compassionate but professional]

"Take your time.

[Push tissue box toward them]

[SILENCE - 30-60 seconds. Let them cry. Don't rush.]

Я знаю this is incredibly hard.

[Wait until они compose slightly]

Когда вы ready, мы можем continue. Нет rush.

[If they're ready]

Я хочу, чтобы вы знали - вы будете OK. Этот severance package provides
[X months] runway, plus outplacement support. You have time.

Ваши skills и experience are valuable. Вы найдете another great opportunity."
```

**Key Principles:**
- **EMPATHY**: Don't be cold, это human being in pain
- **PATIENCE**: Дайте время compose, не rush
- **REASSURANCE**: They WILL be OK (but not false promises)
- **PRACTICAL**: Reference support (severance, outplacement)

### Reaction Type 3: Negotiation / Bargaining

**Scenario:**
```
Executive: "OK, I hear you. But can we do 12 months severance instead of 6?
And full equity acceleration? I have 2 kids in college..."
```

**Response Script:**

```
CEO: "Я understand вы'd like more. Давайте я note ваши requests.

[Write down specific requests]

- 12 months severance (current offer: 6)
- Full equity acceleration (current: 25%)
- [Any other asks]

Вот что я могу do:

1. Please review полный package с вашим attorney
2. If они have specific concerns или counter-proposals, have them contact
   [Legal contact]
3. Я cannot commit к changes right now - любые изменения require
   [Board approval / legal review]
4. Мы открыты для reasonable discussion

Но хочу быть clear: Базовый package уже [competitive / per contract /
generous]. Significant changes unlikely, но мы listen."
```

**DO NOT:**
- Agree to anything on the spot (даже если кажется reasonable)
- Set precedent (other execs узнают о deal)
- Negotiate without Board/Legal approval

**Common Negotiation Requests & Responses:**

```
Request: "Can I have 3 months to find a new role before leaving?"

Response: "Current effective date is [date]. Но мы можем discuss
[consulting agreement] где вы available для knowledge transfer и
это extends ваш timeline. Давайте explore эту option."

Request: "Can you write me a strong recommendation letter?"

Response: "Я willing provide [neutral / positive] reference при
reference checks. Regarding formal letter, давайте discuss после
вы review separation agreement. Мы можем include language о
references там."

Request: "Can you remove the non-compete?"

Response: "Non-compete is part вашего original employment contract.
Removing it is significant change. Но давайте ваш attorney discuss
с [Legal]. Мы can consider если есть legitimate concerns."
```

### Reaction Type 4: Denial / Shock

**Scenario:**
```
Executive: [Stares blankly]
"This can't be happening. There must be some mistake. Just last month
you said I was doing great..."
```

**Response Script:**

```
CEO: "Я know это surprising. Давайте я address ваш concern.

[Re: 'You said I was doing great last month']

В last month's conversation, я mentioned [positive thing]. Это was true
для того context. Но это decision базировалась на [longer timeframe /
Board assessment / specific issues that emerged].

Это not mistake. Решение carefully considered by [Board / leadership team]
over [timeframe].

Я understand вы may not agree с decision. Это ваше right. Но decision is
final.

Давайте focus на transition и ensuring вы have support going forward."
```

**Key Principles:**
- Acknowledge shock (valid feeling)
- Don't defend every past statement (context changes)
- Reaffirm finality (не give false hope)
- Redirect to practical next steps

### Reaction Type 5: Professionalism / Acceptance

**Scenario:**
```
Executive: [Takes deep breath]
"OK. I'm disappointed, but I understand. Let's talk through the details."
```

**Response Script:**

```
CEO: "Спасибо за professionalism. Это appreciated.

[Proceed with severance details and logistics]

Я want вас знать - ваши contributions к [Company] were significant:
- [Specific achievement 1]
- [Specific achievement 2]

Это not negated by этого decision.

We're committed к supporting вашего transition. Давайте make sure вы
have everything needed для successful next chapter."
```

**Key Principles:**
- Acknowledge and appreciate professionalism
- Reiterate contributions (end on respectful note)
- Proceed efficiently with logistics
- Offer genuine support

## Post-Meeting Actions

### Immediate Actions (Within 30 Minutes)

```
□ DOCUMENT CONVERSATION
  - Who was present
  - What was said (key points and reactions)
  - Any threats, unusual reactions, or commitments made
  - Time meeting ended
  → Send summary to Legal immediately

□ IT ACCESS REVOCATION
  - Email disabled
  - Slack/collaboration tools disabled
  - VPN access removed
  - Systems access (GitHub, AWS, databases, etc.) removed
  - Calendar wiped of future meetings
  → Confirm with IT: "Access disabled for [Name]"

□ PHYSICAL SECURITY
  - Badge deactivated
  - Building access removed
  - Parking access removed
  - Remote access (if applicable) disabled

□ RETRIEVE COMPANY PROPERTY
  - Laptop, phone, tablet
  - Security badge, access keys
  - Company credit card
  - Any other equipment
  → Document what was returned (checklist)

□ SECURE ACCOUNTS & DATA
  - Change passwords for shared accounts
  - Transfer ownership of documents, code, projects
  - Revoke admin privileges
  - Archive their email (legal hold if applicable)

□ NOTIFY KEY PEOPLE (In Order)
  1. Executive team (within 1 hour)
  2. Board (C-level exits, within 2 hours)
  3. Direct reports (within 2-4 hours, via 1-on-1s)
  4. Broader organization (within 24 hours, via announcement)
```

### First 24 Hours

```
□ COMMUNICATION DEPLOYMENT

  Hour 0-4: Executive Team
  - Brief in person or video call
  - Share agreed messaging
  - Answer questions
  - Appoint interim leader (if needed)

  Hour 4-8: Direct Reports
  - CEO or interim leader meets 1-on-1 with each direct report
  - Use script:

    "I want to share that [Name] is leaving [Company] effective [date].
    This was [business decision / performance decision / strategic change].

    [Interim leader] will be leading the team going forward. Your role
    and priorities remain the same.

    I know you may have questions. What I can share is [approved messaging].

    What questions do you have?"

  - Assess loyalty (will they leave? are they upset?)
  - Reassure stability

  Hour 8-24: Company-Wide
  - CEO email to all employees (see Communication Templates)
  - Department All-Hands (if C-level exit)
  - Slack announcement (pin in #general)

□ EXTERNAL COMMUNICATION (If Applicable)

  Public Company:
  - Draft SEC Form 8-K filing (within 4 business days)
  - Prepare press release
  - Brief investor relations
  - Coordinate with PR team

  Private Company:
  - Notify Board (email summary)
  - Inform investors (if significant exit)
  - Customer communication (if customer-facing role)

□ LEGAL & HR ADMINISTRATION

  - Separation agreement sent via DocuSign
  - COBRA paperwork prepared
  - Final paycheck processed (state law compliance)
  - Benefits termination coordinated
  - Exit interview scheduled

□ TRANSITION PLANNING

  - Interim leader designated (if not already)
  - Critical projects identified and reassigned
  - Customer/partner handoffs planned
  - Knowledge transfer initiated (if consulting agreement)
  - Executive search initiated (if C-level)
```

### First Week

```
□ TEAM STABILITY ASSESSMENT
  - Monitor for resignations (domino effect)
  - 1-on-1s with key team members
  - Address rumors and gossip
  - Reinforce stability messaging

□ CUSTOMER/PARTNER COMMUNICATION
  - Identify accounts at risk (if sales/customer-facing role)
  - Proactive outreach: "Seamless transition, no impact"
  - Introduce interim leader or account owner

□ BOARD UPDATE (C-Level Exits)
  - Formal written update
  - Transition plan and timeline
  - Search strategy for replacement
  - Business continuity assessment

□ POST-MORTEM (HR + CEO)
  - What went well in termination process?
  - What could be improved?
  - Any legal risks that emerged?
  - Update termination playbook

□ MONITOR SOCIAL MEDIA
  - Watch departed exec's LinkedIn, Twitter
  - Ensure compliance with non-disparagement
  - Coordinate public posts (if agreed)
```

### 30-60 Days Post-Termination

```
□ SEPARATION AGREEMENT STATUS
  - Signed and returned?
  - Any negotiations or disputes?
  - Severance payments initiated?

□ KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER
  - Consulting agreement executed (if applicable)
  - Critical knowledge documented
  - Team ramped up on exec's former responsibilities

□ REPLACEMENT SEARCH
  - Executive search firm engaged (if C-level)
  - Job description and candidate profile defined
  - Initial candidates interviewed

□ TEAM PERFORMANCE
  - Assess team morale and productivity
  - Identify any performance impacts
  - Course correct if needed

□ LEGAL RISKS
  - Any threatening letters from exec's attorney?
  - Litigation filed?
  - Compliance with separation agreement?
```

## Special Scenarios

### Scenario: Executive Refuses to Leave Room

```
Executive: "I'm not leaving until you change this decision."

Response:

CEO: "I understand you're upset. But the meeting is over, and this
decision is final.

You have two options:

1. Leave now, and we'll schedule follow-up tomorrow to discuss logistics
2. If you refuse, I'll need to ask Security to escort you

Which do you prefer?"

[If they still refuse]

CEO: "[Security], please escort [Name] from the building."
```

### Scenario: Executive Threatens to Take Clients/Employees

```
Executive: "Fine. I'm leaving. And I'm taking the Sales team and half our
customers with me."

Response:

CEO: "I'll stop you right there. Your employment contract includes
non-solicit provisions.

If you violate those terms:
- You forfeit severance
- We will pursue legal action
- You will be liable for damages

I strongly advise you speak with your attorney before taking any actions.

This conversation is over."

[Document threats immediately, send to Legal]

```
Executive: "I just want to understand. What specifically went wrong?

Response:

CEO: "That's a fair question. Here's honest feedback:

[If performance-based, give 2-3 specific examples]
- [Example 1 with context and impact]
- [Example 2]

[If culture fit]

feedback. It's respectful and helps them grow.

## Appendix: Conversation Preparation Checklist

### 72 Hours Before

```
□ Legal review of all documents
□ Board approval obtained (if required)
□ Separation agreement finalized
□ Severance amount confirmed and budgeted
□ Communication plan drafted (internal, external)
□ IT and Security briefed
□ Interim leader identified and briefed
□ Transition plan drafted
```

### 24 Hours Before

```
□ Final go/no-go decision
□ Meeting room reserved
□ Participants confirmed (CEO, HR, Legal)
□ Materials printed and ready
□ Script reviewed by CEO
□ Role-play with HR (practice difficult reactions)
□ Security on standby (if needed)
□ IT ready to disable access
```

### Day Of (Morning)

```
□ CEO mental preparation (this is hard, stay composed)
□ Final review of separation agreement
□ Confirm IT and Security coordination
□ Materials in meeting room
□ Final check: Is this the right decision? (Yes → proceed)
```

### During Meeting

```
□ Stay calm and composed (even if they react badly)
□ Stick to script (don't ad-lib, especially on severance)
□ Don't negotiate (defer to legal review)
□ Show empathy without weakness
□ Be clear on finality
□ Document key points and reactions
```

### After Meeting

```
□ Debrief with HR and Legal
□ Document conversation in detail
□ Execute post-meeting actions (IT, Security, communications)
□ Monitor for risks (legal threats, social media)
□ Support transition and team stability
```

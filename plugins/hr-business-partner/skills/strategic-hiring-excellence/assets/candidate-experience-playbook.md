# Candidate Experience Playbook

## Moments that Matter
1. Application acknowledgement (SLA 24h).
2. Pre-brief (ролевая информация, interview panel).
3. Interview day logistics (agenda, breaks, feedback).
4. Post-interview follow-up (≤48h).
5. Offer storytelling (mission, impact, growth).

## Metrics
- Candidate NPS, drop-off %, offer acceptance %, time between stages.

## Recovery
- Escalation path для негативного опыта, компенсационные gestures (gift cards, personalized notes).

# Hiring Operating Cadence

- **Weekly**: Recruiting sync (pipeline health, blockers).
- **Bi-weekly**: Hiring Committee review (offers, exceptions, DEI metrics).
- **Monthly**: Bar Raiser retro, time-to-fill trends, interviewer capacity.
- **Quarterly**: Workforce plan refresh, tooling review, branding initiatives.

Используйте документ как шаблон для записи решений и owners.

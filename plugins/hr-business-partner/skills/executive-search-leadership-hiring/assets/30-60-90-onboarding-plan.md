# 30-60-90 Onboarding Plan (Executive)

## 0-30 Days
- Stakeholder tour (Board, ELT, key customers).
- Org + product immersion, read docs, run listening tour.
- Deliverable: Diagnostic memo (wins, risks, opportunities).

## 31-60 Days
- Align strategy with CEO/Board, design operating cadence, define KPIs.
- Early talent moves (key hires, role clarity).
- Deliverable: 3-year vision + 12-month execution plan.

## 61-90 Days
- Launch quick-win initiatives (2-3 high-impact projects).
- Finalize org design, budgets, instrumentation.
- Deliverable: Board update + hiring roadmap.

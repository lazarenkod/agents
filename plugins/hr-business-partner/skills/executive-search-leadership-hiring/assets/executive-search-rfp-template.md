# Executive Search RFP Template

## Overview
- Role, level, scope, urgency, confidentiality level.

## Requirements
- Candidate profile summary, success metrics, diversity goals.
- Timeline expectations, reporting cadence, onsite requirements.

## Deliverables
- Longlist cadence, weekly pipeline report, candidate briefs, reference notes.

## Commercials
- Fee structure (retainer %, success fee), guarantee period, expense policy.

## Evaluation
- Firm expertise, recent placements, references, conflict checks.

## Governance
- Communication protocol, data security, NDA, escalation path.

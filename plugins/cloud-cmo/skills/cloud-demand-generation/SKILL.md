# Cloud Demand Generation

## When to Use This Skill

- Разработать demand generation стратегию для нового сервиса
- Оптимизировать lead generation funnel (MQL → SQL → Opportunity)
- Спланировать multi-channel campaigns (SEO, SEM, Content, ABM)
- Настроить lead scoring и qualification
- Улучшить conversion rates на каждом этапе funnel
- Рассчитать и оптимизировать CAC по каналам

## Core Concepts

### 1. Demand Gen Funnel для Cloud

```
TOFU (Top of Funnel) - Awareness:
├── Goal: Привлечь целевую аудиторию
├── Channels: SEO, Content, Social, Display Ads
├── Metrics: Traffic, Impressions, Reach
├── Content: Blog posts, tutorials, thought leadership
└── Cost: Low CPM, high volume

MOFU (Middle of Funnel) - Consideration:
├── Goal: Educate и nurture prospects
├── Channels: Email, Webinars, Retargeting, LinkedIn
├── Metrics: Engagement, Downloads, Trial Signups
├── Content: Whitepapers, case studies, comparison guides
└── Lead Scoring: MQL qualification criteria

BOFU (Bottom of Funnel) - Decision:
├── Goal: Convert to paying customers
├── Channels: Sales outreach, Demos, Free trials
├── Metrics: SQLs, Opportunities, Closed-Won
├── Content: ROI calculators, pricing info, customer proof
└── Sales Handoff: Qualified leads → Sales team

Post-Sale - Expansion:
├── Goal: Retain и expand accounts
├── Channels: CSM outreach, In-product prompts, Email
├── Metrics: NRR, Expansion ARR, Churn rate
├── Content: Best practices, new features, upsell offers
└── Account Growth: Cross-sell, upsell opportunities
```

### 2. Channel Mix Strategy

#### SEO (Organic Search) - Foundation
```
Strategy:
├── Target: High-intent keywords (tutorials, comparisons, pricing)
├── Content: 200+ technical tutorials, product docs, guides
├── Timeline: 6-12 months to see results
├── Investment: $100K-300K/year (content production)
├── ROI: Highest long-term ROI (3-5x)

Key Metrics:
├── Organic traffic: 100K+ monthly visitors (target)
├── Keyword rankings: Top 3 for target keywords
├── Conversion rate: 3-5% (visitor to signup)
├── CAC: $50-100 (lowest cost channel)
└── Content performance: 10+ articles driving 1K+ monthly visits each

Tactics:
├── Technical content (tutorials, how-tos)
├── Comparison pages (vs AWS, Azure, GCP)
├── Product documentation (comprehensive, searchable)
├── Guest posting (industry publications)
└── Link building (earn backlinks from authority sites)
```

#### SEM (Paid Search) - Acceleration
```
Strategy:
├── Target: Commercial intent keywords (pricing, alternatives, vs competitor)
├── Budget: $50K-500K/month depending on scale
├── Platform: Google Ads primarily, Bing secondary
├── Timeline: Immediate results
├── ROI: 2-4x (short-term, scalable)

Campaign Structure:
├── Brand defense: 100% impression share на brand terms ($5-20 CPC)
├── Competitive: "AWS alternative", "Azure vs [us]" ($50-200 CPC)
├── Product: "Managed Kubernetes", "Serverless hosting" ($30-100 CPC)
├── Generic: "Cloud hosting", "Cloud platform" ($20-80 CPC)
└── Remarketing: Previous website visitors ($10-30 CPC)

Optimization:
├── Bidding: Target ROAS 3-5x (SMB), 5-10x (Enterprise leads)
├── Ad copy: Test multiple variations (A/B testing)
├── Landing pages: Dedicated pages per keyword group
├── Conversion tracking: Signups, trials, demo requests
└── Negative keywords: Filter out job seekers, students, unqualified
```

#### Content Marketing - Education
```
Blog Strategy:
├── Frequency: 3-5 posts per week
├── Mix: 50% technical tutorials, 20% product updates, 20% thought leadership, 10% customer stories
├── Distribution: Email, social, syndication (Dev.to, Medium)
├── Goal: 100K+ monthly blog visitors, 5K+ email subscribers

Webinar Program:
├── Frequency: 2-4 webinars per month
├── Types: Product demos, thought leadership, customer panels
├── Promotion: Email, LinkedIn, Twitter, paid ads
├── Goal: 500-1000 registrants per webinar, 40% attendance rate
├── Follow-up: Recording, slides, nurture sequence

Gated Content (Lead Magnets):
├── Whitepapers: Technical deep-dives (15-30 pages)
├── eBooks: Comprehensive guides (30-50 pages)
├── Research reports: Industry benchmarks, surveys
├── Templates: Architecture diagrams, checklists
└── Goal: 100-500 downloads/month, MQL conversion 30%+
```

#### Account-Based Marketing (ABM) - Enterprise
```
Strategy:
├── Target: Fortune 1000 accounts ($100K+ ACV potential)
├── Lists: 100-500 target accounts (hand-selected)
├── Channels: LinkedIn, Display, Email, Direct mail
├── Investment: $50K-200K per quarter
├── Timeline: 6-12 month sales cycles
├── ROI: 10-20x (high ACV deals)

Tactics:
├── LinkedIn ABM: Sponsored content, InMail to decision makers
├── Display advertising: Retarget employees from target companies
├── Personalized outreach: Custom emails, direct mail, gifting
├── Executive events: Dinners, roundtables, VIP experiences
├── Custom content: Industry-specific whitepapers, case studies
└── Account intelligence: Monitor signals (funding, hiring, tech changes)

Playbook:
1. Identify accounts (ICP match, buyer intent signals)
2. Research key stakeholders (CTO, VP Eng, Cloud Architect)
3. Personalized campaigns (tailored messaging, content)
4. Multi-touch engagement (6-12 touches across channels)
5. Sales handoff (when engagement threshold met)
6. Close-loop feedback (win/loss analysis)

Metrics:
├── Account engagement: 40%+ of target accounts engaged
├── Pipeline influenced: $5M+ in ABM-sourced pipeline
├── Win rate: 35%+ on ABM accounts (vs 25% baseline)
├── CAC: $5K-20K (justified by $100K+ ACV)
└── Sales cycle: 20% shorter than non-ABM
```

### 3. Lead Management

#### Lead Scoring Model
```
Demographic Scoring (Firmographic):
├── Company size: Enterprise (25 pts), Mid-Market (15 pts), SMB (5 pts)
├── Industry: Target verticals (20 pts), Other (5 pts)
├── Role: Decision maker (25 pts), Influencer (15 pts), Individual contributor (5 pts)
├── Geography: Primary markets (10 pts), Secondary (5 pts)
└── Company revenue: $100M+ (15 pts), $10M-100M (10 pts), <$10M (5 pts)

Behavioral Scoring (Engagement):
├── Website visits: 5+ pages (10 pts), 10+ pages (20 pts)
├── Content downloads: Whitepaper (15 pts), Case study (10 pts), Blog (5 pts)
├── Webinar: Registered (10 pts), Attended (20 pts)
├── Trial signup: Free tier (30 pts), Paid trial (50 pts)
├── Pricing page: Visited (15 pts), Calculator used (25 pts)
├── Email engagement: Opened (5 pts), Clicked (10 pts)
└── Product usage: Logged in (20 pts), Deployed resource (40 pts)

MQL Threshold:
├── Score 70+: Marketing Qualified Lead (pass to sales)
├── Score 40-69: Nurture (email campaigns, retargeting)
├── Score <40: Top-of-funnel (broad content, education)

Decay:
├── No activity 30 days: -20% score
├── No activity 60 days: -50% score
├── No activity 90 days: Reset to 0 (re-engage campaign)
```

#### Lead Nurture Programs
```
Early Stage Nurture (Score 20-40):
├── Duration: 12 weeks
├── Frequency: 1 email per week
├── Content: Educational (tutorials, guides, industry trends)
├── Goal: Increase engagement score to 40+
└── Success: 15-20% progress to mid-stage

Mid-Stage Nurture (Score 40-69):
├── Duration: 8 weeks
├── Frequency: 2 emails per week
├── Content: Product-focused (features, use cases, comparisons)
├── CTAs: Webinar registration, free trial, demo request
├── Goal: Convert to MQL (70+ score)
└── Success: 25-30% convert to MQL

MQL Nurture (Score 70+, не yet SQL):
├── Duration: 4 weeks
├── Frequency: 3 touches per week (email, LinkedIn, retargeting)
├── Content: Decision-stage (ROI, pricing, customer stories)
├── CTAs: Schedule demo, contact sales, pricing consultation
├── Goal: Sales-accept (SQL)
└── Success: 40-50% accept by sales

Trial Nurture (Free trial users):
├── Day 1: Welcome email + quick start guide
├── Day 3: Tutorial (deploy first resource)
├── Day 7: Use case examples (similar companies)
├── Day 14: Check-in (need help? support resources)
├── Day 21: Upgrade prompt (limits, premium features)
├── Day 28: Expiry warning (special offer, talk to sales)
└── Goal: 20-30% trial-to-paid conversion
```

### 4. Conversion Rate Optimization

```
Website Optimization:
├── Homepage: 3-5% visitor-to-signup conversion (benchmark)
├── Pricing page: 8-12% visitor-to-trial conversion
├── Product pages: 2-4% visitor-to-trial conversion
├── Landing pages: 15-25% PPC traffic conversion (highly targeted)

Key Experiments:
├── Hero headline (value prop clarity)
├── CTA copy ("Start Free Trial" vs "Get Started" vs "Try for Free")
├── CTA placement (above fold, sticky footer, sidebar)
├── Social proof (logos, testimonials, user count)
├── Pricing display (tiers, calculator, comparison)
├── Form length (email only vs email+name+company)
├── Trust signals (security badges, certifications, uptime)

Trial Activation:
├── Signup to first login: 85%+ (reduce friction)
├── Login to first resource: 70%+ (onboarding quality)
├── First resource to "aha moment": 60%+ (product value)
├── Aha moment to payment: 25-30% (conversion optimization)

Landing Page Template:
├── Above fold: Value prop + CTA + Social proof + Hero image
├── Benefits section: 3-5 key benefits with icons
├── How it works: 3-step process
├── Social proof: Customer logos + testimonial
├── Features: Key differentiators vs competition
├── Pricing: Transparent pricing or "View Pricing" CTA
├── FAQ: Address common objections
├── Final CTA: Repeat strong call-to-action
```

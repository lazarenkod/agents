## –®–∞–≥ 5: Customer Research

### Customer Interview Plan

**Target**: _______ interviews (recommended: 15-30 for qualitative insights)

**Participant Criteria**:
- [ ] Current customers (understand why they chose us)
- [ ] Prospects (understand evaluation criteria)
- [ ] Competitor customers (understand why they chose them)
- [ ] Target ICP (validate assumptions)

**Recruitment**:
- [ ] Customer advisory board members
- [ ] Trial users (who didn't convert - why?)
- [ ] Sales pipeline contacts (prospects in evaluation)
- [ ] LinkedIn outreach (cold outreach to ICP)
- [ ] User research panel (dedicated panel, incentives)

**Incentive**: $_______ (gift card, credits, etc.) per interview

### Interview Guide

**Interview Structure** (45-60 minutes):

1. **Introduction** (5 min):
   - Explain purpose
   - Assure confidentiality
   - Get permission to record

2. **Background** (10 min):
   - Role, responsibilities
   - Company, industry
   - Current cloud setup

3. **Needs & Pain Points** (15 min):
   - What are you trying to achieve with cloud?
   - What challenges do you face?
   - What frustrates you about current solution?
   - What would make your life easier?

4. **Decision Process** (10 min):
   - How did you choose your current cloud provider?
   - Who was involved in decision?
   - What criteria did you evaluate?
   - How long did it take?

5. **Product/Solution Fit** (15 min):
   - [If current customer] Why did you choose us?
   - [If prospect] What would make you switch?
   - [If competitor customer] What keeps you with them?
   - What features are most important?
   - What features are missing?

6. **Pricing** (5 min):
   - How much do you spend on cloud?
   - Is price a concern?
   - What pricing model do you prefer?
   - What would you pay for [our solution]?

7. **Wrap-up** (5 min):
   - Any other thoughts?
   - Can we follow up?
   - Thank you!

# Sales Methods Cheatsheet
- **MEDDPICC:** Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Decision Process, Paper, Pain, Champion, Competition.
- **Challenger:** Teach/Tailor/Take Control; commercial insight; constructive tension.
- **SPIN:** Situation/Problem/Implication/Need-Payoff вопросы.
- **Strategic Selling:** Red/Yellow/Green flags, buying influences (economic/technical/user/coach).
- **BANT (слабее):** Budget/Authority/Need/Timeline — не хватает конкурентного/рискового слоя.

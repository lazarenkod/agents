# Innovation Portfolio Management

## Когда использовать

- Приоритизация R&D инвестиций
- Управление innovation pipeline
- Оценка emerging technologies
- Планирование technology roadmap
- Balancing innovation vs maintenance

## Three Horizons Framework

```markdown
# McKinsey 3 Horizons Model

## Horizon 1: Core Business (70% resources)
**Timeframe**: 0-12 months
**Focus**: Extend и defend core business
**Risk**: Low
**Examples**:
- Feature enhancements
- Performance optimization
- Bug fixes
- Technical debt paydown

## Horizon 2: Emerging Opportunities (20% resources)
**Timeframe**: 1-3 years
**Focus**: Build emerging businesses
**Risk**: Medium
**Examples**:
- New service categories
- Adjacent markets
- Platform extensions
- Strategic partnerships

## Horizon 3: Future Options (10% resources)
**Timeframe**: 3-5 years
**Focus**: Create viable options
**Risk**: High
**Examples**:
- Disruptive technologies
- New business models
- Moonshot projects
- R&D experiments
```

## Innovation Metrics

```yaml
Input Metrics:
  - R&D spend: % of revenue
  - Innovation headcount: FTEs
  - PoCs initiated: count/quarter

Process Metrics:
  - Time to PoC: days
  - PoC success rate: %
  - Prototype to pilot: conversion rate

Output Metrics:
  - New services launched: count/year
  - Revenue from new services: $
  - Patents filed: count
  - Customer adoption: users

Impact Metrics:
  - Revenue contribution: % from H2/H3 initiatives
  - Market share gains: %
  - Customer satisfaction: NPS
  - Technology leadership: analyst recognition
```
